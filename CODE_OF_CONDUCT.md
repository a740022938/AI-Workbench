# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

We want AI Workbench to be a respectful, constructive, and technically serious collaboration space.

Participants are expected to:
- communicate respectfully
- give actionable feedback
- focus on facts, reproduction steps, and real improvement
- disagree on ideas without attacking people
- keep discussions useful for maintainers and contributors

## Unacceptable Behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:
- personal attacks or harassment
- hostile or insulting language
- repeated bad-faith arguments
- spam or intentionally disruptive behavior
- publishing private or sensitive information without permission

## Project Context

This repository is currently in a **Technical Preview / Stage Release** state.
That means:
- rough edges may still exist
- maintainers may make strict scope decisions
- not every request will be accepted immediately

Please keep feedback grounded, specific, and technically useful.

## Reporting Problems

If you experience unacceptable behavior, report it privately to the repository maintainer.
For security-related issues, follow `SECURITY.md` instead of public discussion.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close threads, or restrict participation when behavior becomes harmful to the project or community.
